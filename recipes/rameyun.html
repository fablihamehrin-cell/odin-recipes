Rameyun

The Ultimate Comfort Ramyun

This isn't just basic instant noodles. It’s a rich, spicy, and deeply savory bowl that feels like a hug. It's all about the toppings and the perfectly timed eggs.

Ramen Recipe

Ingredients

The Base

  • 1 pack Korean instant ramyun (like Shin Ramyun or Neoguri)
  • 2.5 cups water (or beef broth for extra richness)
  • 150g flank steak or ribeye, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kimchi, chopped

The Essentials

  • 1 or 2 eggs (for those jammy yolks)
  • 2 stalks green onions, thinly sliced on a diagonal
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil

The Signature Spice

  • The included soup base packet
  • 1/2 tsp Gochugaru (Korean chili flakes)
  • 1 tsp Soy sauce

Steps :

  1. The Jammy Eggs: Bring a small pot of water to a boil. Gently lower the eggs in and boil for exactly 6 minutes and 30 seconds. Immediately move them to an ice bath. Once cool, peel them carefully and slice in half.
  2. Sear the Beef: In your main pot, heat the oil over high heat. Add the beef strips and sear them quickly until browned. Toss in the minced garlic and kimchi, and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. The Broth: Pour in the water (or broth). Add the ramyun soup base and the extra gochugaru and soy sauce. Bring everything to a rolling boil.
  4. The Noodles: Add the noodle block. Cook for about 3–4 minutes. Tip: Lift the noodles into the air with tongs a few times while they cook; the contact with air makes them extra chewy!
  5. Final Simmer: Turn off the heat just before the noodles are fully soft, as they will keep cooking in the bowl.
How to craft the bowl
  1. The Pour: Pour the noodles and the spicy broth into a deep, aesthetic ceramic bowl. Make sure the beef and kimchi are visible on top.
  2. The Arrangement: Place your halved soft-boiled eggs on one side. The bright yellow yolk against the red broth looks incredible for photos.
  3. The Garnish: Pile the sliced green onions in the center. Sprinkle a few extra chili flakes or sesame seeds if you want that extra detail.
  4. Serve: Serve immediately while it's steaming hot!
